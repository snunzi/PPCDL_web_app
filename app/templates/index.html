Welcome to PPCDL Bioinformatics Web GUI.

Please follow directions below to upload your samples and run analysis

  1. Go to 'My Runs' and select 'Add New Run'
  2. Enter metadata and upload fastq files
  3. Check the run you want to analyze and click 'Analyze'
  4. Start the analysis and you will recieve an email when the pipeline finishes and your results are ready to view
  5. Results can be viewed on your web browser or downloaded to your home directory
